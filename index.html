About us

Agyare Real Estate and Construction Ghana Limited has been in operation since 2011 but was formally incorporated in the year 2017 as a limited liability company under the company’s Act 1963,179 with its office located at Kasoa high tension. It was established to serve its clientele Estate land acquisition, Estate housing, Land registration and Consultation, Land management and General Constructions.

  • MISSION

    • Our mission as a company is to help individuals, corporate institutions and the government/ state institutions to acquire fully documented serviced plots of land

  • VISION

    • To achieve the highest possible standards in the Real Estate industry thereby helping individuals own a secured land and have an ease in creating property/(ies) on them.

  • VALUE POSITIONING

    • Totally, we are committed to working in partnership with our clients and focusing on satisfying the interest in Real Estate development. We are committed to delivering on our services, provide a result-oriented support and focus on excellence.
